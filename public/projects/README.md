# Projects Folder

Drop your project images into the appropriate subfolders:

## Residential Projects
- `residential/interior/` - Interior design photos
- `residential/elevation/` - Building facade/exterior photos
- `residential/landscape/` - Garden and outdoor area photos

## Commercial Projects
- `commercial/interior/` - Office/retail interior photos
- `commercial/elevation/` - Commercial building exterior photos
- `commercial/landscape/` - Commercial outdoor area photos

## Supported Formats
`.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`, `.gif`

## Tips
- Use descriptive filenames (they become titles): `modern-office-delhi.jpg`
- Refresh the website after adding images
